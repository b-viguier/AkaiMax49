# PCB

## Center Pannel Board

FFC `A`, 30 pins:
```
 1 3 5 7 9 b d f h j l n p r t
0 2 4 6 8 a c e g i k m o q s
```

FFC `B`, 26 pins:
```
 1 3 5 7 9 b d f h j l n p
0 2 4 6 8 a c e g i k m o
```


# `B` addressing

⚠️ Don't forget `B-7` is the clock. 

| `a` | `9` | `b` | `c` | `d` | `e` | `f` | `g` | Target |  
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | ------ |
|  1  |  1  |  X  |  X  |  X  |  X  |  X  |  X  | None   |

* `a` : buttons
* `9` : LEDs
